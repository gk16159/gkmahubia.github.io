About My Research


I investigate phase transitions, elastic anomalies, phonon instabilities and thermodynamic behavior of Earth and planetary minerals under extreme pressure-temperature conditions. My work bridges computational simulations with Raman and X-ray diffraction experiments.

Research Experience

  • Computing elastic constants, phonon dispersion and thermodynamic properties using Phonopy and QHA.
