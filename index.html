About Me

I am a computational Earth scientist focusing on high-pressure mineral physics, planetary materials, and first-principles simulations. My research is centered on understanding phase transitions, elastic behavior, vibrational anomalies, and thermodynamic stability of minerals under deep Earth and exoplanetary interior conditions. I work extensively with Quantum ESPRESSO, Phonopy, quasi-harmonic approximation, ElaStic, Mineral-CIJ, EOS fitting, Gibbs2 and Python-based automation integrated with experimental Raman and powder X-ray diffraction observations.
